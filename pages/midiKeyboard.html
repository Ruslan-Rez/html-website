Midi Keyboard

A MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) keyboard is an electronic device that resembles a traditional piano keyboard but doesn't produce sound on its own. Instead, it sends MIDI data to a computer or a sound module, allowing you to control virtual instruments and software synthesizers.
MIDI keyboards are essential for music production as they enable composers, producers, and musicians to input musical notes and control various aspects of their virtual instruments and software. They offer a more intuitive and expressive way to create melodies, chords, and other musical elements, enhancing the overall creative process.
