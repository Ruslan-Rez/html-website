DAW(Digital Audio Workstation)

A Digital Audio Workstation is software designed for recording, editing, arranging, and mixing digital audio. It provides a platform for music production, offering a wide range of tools and features to create professional-level tracks
A DAW is the backbone of music production. It allows you to record audio, import MIDI data, manipulate sounds, apply effects, arrange the composition, and mix the final output. With a DAW, you have the power to turn your musical ideas into fully-fledged songs and productions.
